Trading

Trading in Blockchain comes in different form but similar. Trading happens in cryptocurrency,NFTs and forex



Trading in cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency trading means taking a financial position on the price direction of individual cryptocurrencies against the dollar (in crypto/dollar pairs) or against another crypto,via crypto to crypto pairs. This means it is a process of having a financial stand when it comes to buying in dollars, or exchanging with another coin using its value

And while CFDs (contracts for difference) are a particularly popular way to trade cryptocurrencies as they allow greater flexibility, the use of leverage and the ability to take short as well as long position



How to trade NFTs

First of all, you can't buy and sell NFTs on the same exchange where you got your crypto.To put your ETH to work you will have to join a marketplace where people showcase,buy,sell and trade NFTs(work of arts, pictures, media content etc.). Then you will link your digital wallet-crypto and all- to your new NFTs marketplace account.

Nevertheless, trust wallet,metamask and coinbase supports trading NFTs and remember you need crypto to venture into NFTs trading



Forex trading

Similar to crypto trading but first step is to educate yourself about the market's operations and terminology. Next, you to develop a trading strategy based on your finances and risk tolerance. Finally, you should open a brokerage account. Today,it is easier than ever to open and fund a forex account online and begin trading currencies
